Who Are We

The Austin Tech Crew is a committee of talented Rackers who foster continuation of technical skills and knowledge for all Rackers, and expand the Rackspace name and brand in surrounding tech communities by sponsoring local tech events.

Meetups

Rackspace hosts meetups in our Austin offices. We strive to inject Racker culture into the meetup scene and community by hosting OpenStack, Docker Austin, Cloud Austin, DevOps Austin, Women Who Code, Pyladies and many more...

Sponsorship

Rackspace reaches into the tech communities by sponsoring local tech events like hackathons, resume & interviewing workshops, tech talks, diversity and women tech events. Our purpose is to help promote and further build our Austin tech community.

Fanatical Support for AWS

Members of our elite AWS team bring their expertise and best practices to architect, secure, and operate critical customer workloads on the AWS platform. We also develop our own proprietary tooling to allow our customers to efficiently leverage the power of the AWS feature set. Our AWS practice continues its aggressive global expansion—the importance of identifying, hiring, and retaining top talent who will raise the bar is imperative to our success. Come join us on this mission.

Openstack



Rackspace founded OpenStack with NASA in 2010. We now operate the world’s largest OpenStack cloud and have experience scaling OpenStack clouds to thousands of nodes across public, private, and hybrid environments. Rackspace is pushing the limits on scale, where the most interesting problems of reliability, performance, and complexity are solved. As a result, Rackers are prolific, respected OpenStack contributors and thought leaders in the OpenStack community. If you have a passion and expertise in OpenStack architecture, engineering, or operations, and a desire to create powerful solutions at scale, an OpenStack opportunity at Rackspace is right for you.
